3. Click device toolbar (or `Ctrl+Shift+M`)

## ğŸ“¹ Recording Tips

### For Demo Videos

1. **Script it**: Write down steps before recording
2. **Smooth Mouse**: Move cursor slowly and deliberately
3. **Narration**: Explain what you're doing
4. **Edit**: Cut out waiting times (transaction confirmations)
5. **Music**: Add background music (royalty-free)

### Video Editing Tools

**Free:**
- DaVinci Resolve
